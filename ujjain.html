Ujjain- महाकाल की नगरी

An important spot for Hindu pilgrims to visit in India is the historic city of Ujjain, which is located on the banks of the river Shipra.

Besides serving as the location of the huge Kumbh Mela, Ujjain is also the site of some of the most popular temples, monuments, and other tourist destinations that offer a divine glimpse into the city's rich cultural legacy.

The temples in Ujjain will fascinate anyone, whether they are spiritual people, history buffs, or architecture enthusiasts.

Take a look at these famou temples of Ujjain that you shouldn't miss while visiting this beautiful place with family or friends:-

  1. Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga Temple
  2. Ram Ghat
